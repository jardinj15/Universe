Across The Universe

This webquest is recomended for high school students.

by: Jameson Jardin

A webquest to the universe

In this webquest you will use interactive websites to explore the universe. as a task you will be presented with a worksheet that you will write down 15 interesting facts that can be used later

The big question: How much do you actually know about the universe?

By the end of this webquest you will have a better understanding of the universe you live in and will create an "our map" of the things you've learned.

The "Our map" will be either a powerpoint or a prezi.

  1. First, you will be assigned to a team of three
  2. Then you will be given the worksheet to complete
  3. Next, assign the roles each of you will play
  4. The navigator will follow the links to the resources provided
  5. The writer will record the fifteen facts the group finds together on the website
  6. the imager will find images relating to each fact.
  1. Log scale of the Universe, with clickable objects
  2. an accurate depiction of the solar system based on if the moon were a single pixel
  3. a 3d flythrough of the solar system and other bodies of the universe

By completing this webquest, you will be able to share with the class what you have learned through a Power Point or Prezi. Be sure to share your opinions on what you found
